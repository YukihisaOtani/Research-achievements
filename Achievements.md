# Research Achievements

This repository summarizes selected research achievements in CubeSat systems engineering, spacecraft testing, electrical interface design, mission assurance, and space education.

## Main Research Areas

- CubeSat system design, assembly, integration, verification, and testing
- Spacecraft electrical interfaces and configurable backplane architectures
- Design Structure Matrix analysis and mission assurance
- Failure analysis, risk assessment, and test planning
- Space education and international capacity building
